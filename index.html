Hi! I'm Cookie

Welcome to my website! I was born in a barn, but now I live in a nice home in Boone, NC. I have a mom and dad, and a new cat brother named Pow Pow, and a new dog sister named Grizzle.

I love treats, toys, and trying to escape outside. When I'm not busy playing or biting everything in sight, I also like snuggles. I purr real loud, and I'm working on my big cat ROAR. Mom says my meow is just a little squeak for now, but with hard work and practice it will become real loud.

Mom and dad feed me and play with me everyday. They buy and make me new toys. They can't play as long or as hard as I can, but I am training them seven days a week to be great 'cathletes'. I like to eat right next to brother Pow Pow when it's time to refuel for more fun.
